Cookies

Description

Golden-brown chocolate chip cookies with a crisp edge and a soft, chewy center. These cookies are loaded with gooey chocolate chips, making them the perfect treat for any occasion. The buttery flavor and delightful texture will have everyone coming back for more.

Ingredients:

  • 1 cup (225g) butter, softened
  • 1 cup (200g) sugar
  • 1 cup (200g) brown sugar
  • 2 eggs
  • 2 tsp vanilla extract
  • 3 cups (375g) all-purpose flour
  • 1 tsp baking soda
  • 1/2 tsp salt
  • 2 cups (340g) chocolate chips

Steps:

  1. Preheat your oven to 350°F (175°C).
  2. In a bowl, cream butter, sugar, and brown sugar until fluffy.
  3. Add eggs and vanilla; mix well.
  4. In another bowl, mix flour, baking soda, and salt. Gradually add to the wet ingredients.
  5. Stir in the chocolate chips.
  6. Drop spoonfuls of dough onto a baking sheet.
  7. Bake for 8-10 minutes or until edges are golden.
  8. Let cool slightly before serving.
