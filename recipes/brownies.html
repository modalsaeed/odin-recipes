Brownies

Description

Dense, fudgy brownies with a deep, chocolatey flavor that melts in your mouth. The crackly top and rich, moist interior make these brownies irresistible. Whether enjoyed warm with a scoop of ice cream or on their own, they're the ultimate comfort dessert for chocolate lovers.

Ingredients:

  • 1/2 cup (115g) butter, melted
  • 1 cup (200g) sugar
  • 2 eggs
  • 1 tsp vanilla extract
  • 1/3 cup (40g) unsweetened cocoa powder
  • 1/2 cup (65g) all-purpose flour
  • 1/4 tsp salt
  • 1/4 tsp baking powder

Steps:

  1. Preheat your oven to 350°F (175°C) and grease an 8x8-inch pan.
  2. Mix melted butter, sugar, eggs, and vanilla in a bowl.
  3. Stir in cocoa powder, flour, salt, and baking powder until combined.
  4. Pour batter into the prepared pan and spread evenly.
  5. Bake for 20-25 minutes or until the center is set.
  6. Let cool before slicing into squares.
